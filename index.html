About us

We believe knowledge should never be out of reach.

Girls for Knowledge is an education-focused organization working to remove barriers that prevent girls from learning. Our goal is to connect girls with useful resources, supportive mentors, practical skills, and opportunities to continue their education.

01

Equal access

We support learning opportunities that are inclusive and practical.

Confidence and leadership

Long-term impact

We focus on skills and support that create sustainable change.
